Welcome to your Private Gif Screening

  1. To get started, hover over the "Controller" tab.
  2. Click on a button to pull 10 gifs, or submit your own idea!
  3. If you do submit your own idea, you will notice that it adds this as a button that you can then click!
  4. Favorites can be added with the "Add to Favorites" button, and accessed via the "Favorites" tab.
  5. Clicking on a Gif "In Storage" will move it to "Current Feature" and allow it to play.
  6. To move to the next one, simply click on a new film, one in your favorites, or one on the cutting room floor.
